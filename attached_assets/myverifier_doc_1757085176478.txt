Example Response

Address: The provided email address.
Status: Can be either Valid, Invalid,Catch-all, or Unknown.
Disposable Domain: Indicates if the domain is disposable.
Free Domain: Indicates if the email is not a business address.
Greylisted: The server may not respond properly on the first attempt. Frequent multiple attempts are not advised.
Diagnosis: Provides helpful information about Invalid addresses.
